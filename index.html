AI-Related Hazards

As artificial intelligence becomes more integrated into our daily lives, it also introduces new cybersecurity risks. Malicious actors are leveraging AI to automate attacks, making them faster and more sophisticated than ever before. AI can be used to generate highly convincing deepfake videos or to craft personalized phishing emails on a massive scale. These advanced attacks make it harder to distinguish between legitimate and fraudulent digital content, requiring us to be more vigilant about the authenticity of what we see and hear online.

Ascent

Malware, short for malicious software, is a significant threat to our digital security. This type of software is designed to disrupt computer operations, gather sensitive information, or gain unauthorized access to computer systems.

Common forms include viruses, which attach to legitimate files; worms, which spread independently across networks; and ransomware, which encrypts files and demands payment for their release. Staying safe means being cautious about downloading unfamiliar files, and avoiding suspicious links.
